About the future of the music

Jaribel Sanguino is a 23 year old rising indepent artist. From a very young age she started working on her craft, composing original material, never heard before. But after some time, she joined her brother to start Sanguino Siblings Records, and finally launched her first album: Soy una niña infeliz.

This innovative and melancolic album features Jaribel's first single with the same name: Soy una niña infeliz.

You can listen here to the live presentation of Soy Una Niña Infeliz Single at El Palacio De Bellas Artes, and experience the future of music with your own ears.
